Transform the variation dropdowns on your product pages to Chosen dropdowns.

== Description ==

WooCommerce Chosen Variation Dropdowns does what it says on the tin. WooCommerce Chosen Variation Dropdowns will transform all the variation dropdowns on your product pages to chosen dropdowns allowing your customers to easlity view variations and search form them. The WooCommerce Chosen Variation Dropdowns extension is ideal for shops with large amounts of variations per product, allowing customer to easily filter the variations.

Please feel free to contribute on <a href="https://github.com/kloon/woocommerce-chosen-variation-dropdowns">github</a>.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `woocommerce-chosen-variation-dropdowns` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Done!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where do I disable the search box? =

The search box can be disabled under `WooCommerce -> Settings -> Catalog` selecting the `Disable Product Chosen Search` option and saving the settings.
